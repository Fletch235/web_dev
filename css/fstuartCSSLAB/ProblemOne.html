Tokyo Olympics

Organizers of the Tokyo Olympics pledged that the summer Olympics would be the most eco-friendly games ever.

Here's a list of a few of the things they did to minimize the impact on the environment:

  • All venues were powered with renewable energy
  • Municipal buses were powered by fuel cells
  • They used recycled materials
    1. Uniforms for torchbearers were made with partially recylced Coca-Cola bottles.
    2. Torch was made wih recyled aluminum from temporary housing that built after the earthquake in 2011.
    3. Podiums were 3D printed using plastic trash such as shampoo bottles and were recycled afterwards for packaging for P&G products.
    4. Medals were made from metal from unwanted electronic devices.
  • 90% of the stadiums were electrified
